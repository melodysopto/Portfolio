About
The short version

I'm a product manager who came up through engineering — BSc in Computer Science from the University of Dhaka, then two years managing an engineering team at a Silicon Valley-backed startup before I found my real calling in product.

At Bongo, I've grown from PM to Lead PM across three promotions. Along the way, I've owned everything from subscription funnels and payment integrations to launching live EPL streaming — the kind of work where getting it wrong means millions of people have a bad Friday night.

Struggling to decode tech talk for non-tech folks? That's my jam! Tired of developers constantly nudging you for clarity? Don't worry, I've got your back. I've learned and unlearned, made mistakes, put out fires, and most importantly — re-found my passion for product management.
